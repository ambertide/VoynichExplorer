Voynich Manuscript Project Explore the Manuscript

On 1912, a Polish book dealer came in the possession of mysterious manuscript written in an unknown alphabet and language, a century later, the manuscript that is came to be known by his name remains unsolved.

The Voynich Manuscript is a puzzle, a question that has eluded our best experts so far, after centuries of research it remains unsolved. Many has called it a hoax, merely a creation, and yet, the statistical analysis of the text shows language-like patterns, leading many experts to believe that there is more, hidden beneath the layers, within its pages.

Or perhaps, we are merely obsessed...

What is this project?

The Voynich Explorer is an online explorer you can use to browse the Voynich Manuscript, you can view its pages both in EVA transliteration and in "Voynichese" (daiin), a bunch of characters we have no idea what they mean, but hey, they sure look nice. Clicking on a word will take you to its page, where you can discover where else it appeared on.
